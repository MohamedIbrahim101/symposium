PRUSHLESS PAINTING

INSTRUCTION AND PROTOCOL

1. "The use of brushes is strictly prohibited".
2. Paints, fingers, newsprint, cloth rags, earbuds, and threadbare can all be used to create artwork.
3.Any other supportable equipment other than a brush is permissible.
4. Digital media during painting is not allowed in the nearby vicinity.
5. Students have to bring their own colours and sheets.
6. All types of colours are allowed without brushes
