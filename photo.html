THEME PHOTOGRAPHY

INSTRUCTION AND PROTOCOL

1.The competition is open to all college students.
2.The participants must come with their ID card.
3.Only one entry is allowed per photographer.
4.The photograph must be original and no watermarks of any kind should be on the photograph.
5.The photograph can be in Landscape /Portrait mode.
6.Theme: Open theme.
7.Maximum two photos are allowed to submit.
8.Each photos should contain specific quotes(Tamil or English) or theme name.
9.The photo format should be in JPG format.
10.Google classroom code is given on the spot to upload your photos.
